About us

Who are we?

We love programming robots, creating websites, and teaching others. We work with LEGO MINDSTORMS EV3 and Raspberry Pi.

  • Mission - To inspire other kids to learn programming and robotics
  • Project Highlights - Two of our well-know robotics projects: PIX3L PLOTT3R and EV3Lessons.com.
  • FLL Achievements - We are on FLL Team #51, Not the Droids You Are Looking For .
